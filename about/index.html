About TerraRun

Built by runners, for runners. Free forever, no compromises.

TerraRun is a free, open-source running app that gives you professional-grade analytics without subscriptions or paywalls. We believe running software should be transparent, powerful, and truly free.

Download Now View on GitHub

Our Story

TerraRun started in 2026 when three competitive runners got frustrated with expensive, ad-laden running apps. We believed there had to be a better way—an app built by runners who understand what actually matters when you're out on the trail or pavement.

We spent months researching what runners really want. GPS tracking? Yes. Analytics? Absolutely. But also: no subscriptions, no dark patterns, no selling your location data. Just honest software that works.

Today, TerraRun is trusted by 100k+ runners in 70+ countries. We remain free and independent, supported by the community that built it.
